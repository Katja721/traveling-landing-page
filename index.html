OUR TOP DESTINATIONS

New Zeland

The land of the long white cloud, is famous for its breathtaking landscapes, from lofty mountain peaks and glaciers to mesmerizing blue lakes. Also it is known for its wine, lupins and rugby. As well as its adventure activities and its gorgeous train journeys.

Nepal

Nepal has a diverse geography, including fertile plains, subalpine forested hills, and eight of the world's tallest mountains, including Mount Everest, the highest point on Earth. Nepal is a multi-ethnic, multi-lingual, multi-religious and multi-cultural state, its official language is Nepali.

Norway

Land of the Midnight Sun is famous for its phenomenal fjords, lakes and magical skies. Norway is also famous for its languages, Vikings and folklore, being eco-friendly, and oil production. Also, many inhabitants of Norway are renowned ski fanatics, frozen pizza lovers, and Tesla drivers!
